Park Information

Entrance Fees

  • Adults: $10.50
  • Seniors (65+): free
  • Youth (under 18): $3.00

Activity Fees

  • Zip Lining: $15.00 per person
  • Rock Climbing Gear: $10.00 per person
  • Raft or Canoe Rental: $20.00 for 3 hours/$40.00 for 6 hours

Parking

Parking is free throughout the park

Pets

  • Pets are always welcome
  • Dogs must be on a leash at all times
  • Pet waste stations are provided

Park Hours

  • The Lodge Information Kiosk: 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
  • The Lodge Gift and Supply Shop: 10 a.m. to 5 p.m.
  • Cider Falls Restaurant: 6:30 a.m. to 9:30 p.m.
  • Campground Office: 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
  • Seasonal Food Vendors: 10 a.m. to 3:30 p.m.
